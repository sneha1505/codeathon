T R A C K S

ROUND -1

THE QUIZ ROUND
The first round will be a quiz round where you will be given 30 questions based on the combination of fundamentals of C/C++ and Python from medium to high level and it will be conducted on an online platform with restricted time limit. The first round will be of 15 minutes , each question will be of 30 sec .

ROUND-2

THE DEBUGGING ROUND
:The participants qualifying in the first round , will be eligible for the second ROUND . In ROUND 2, the participants will be given a code with errors, and they need to debug the given code in the given time limit, i.e. 30 minutes.
Whichever team debugs the maximum number of the errors in the given time will be qualified for the last round .

ROUND-3

THE CODING ROUND
In This round the participants will be given a problem statment, and they need to design the code for the same , whichever team designs it first in 1 hour time limit ,will be the winner . As the language admitted for the event will be C/C++/Python and Java so, both the team members in a team should opt for the same language for debugging and Coding rounds .
